From time to time we'll have some news to share with you. It might be to let you know that Micor will be unavailable for maintenance or to let you know about an enhancement to Micor.

The news items' banner appears underneath the Micor logo.

For more information visit Help - Micor news.

​Micor update service

The Micor update service provides a weekly email that lists all of the updates made to Micor in the previous week. If you would like to receive this weekly email, please subscribe.
